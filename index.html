Our Story

Made with Every Stitch of Love

h_crochetnook was born from a simple passion — the rhythmic dance of yarn and hook. Each item we create is a labour of love, made by hand using quality materials that feel as good as they look.

Whether it's a cosy blanket for your living room or a whimsical amigurumi for a little one, every creation tells a story of patience, skill, and warmth.

Meet the Maker

Hi, I'm Hinesha —
your maker from Surat 🌸

"

I'm a student by day and a crocheter by heart. What started as a hobby to de-stress between lectures turned into something I genuinely love — creating little handmade pieces that make people smile. h_crochetnook is my small dream, stitched together one loop at a time.

Based in Surat, Gujarat, I run h_crochetnook as a small home-based business alongside my studies. Every item you see here is made by my own hands — no factories, no shortcuts. From amigurumi animals to cosy blankets, each piece is crafted with patience, passion, and a whole lot of love. Thank you for supporting a student's small business! 💝

Hinesha Shingala 🤍

Founder & Chief Crocheter, h_crochetnook · Surat, Gujarat
